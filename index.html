The Legacy

  • 1989-2007

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our founder, Cody Maverick, was born in Shiverpool, Antarctica. His father passed away at an early age and since then, he had to help provide for his family. He dreamed of making it to Pen Gu Island to start over. In the June of 2007, he made it.

  • June 2007

    PGI Tours is Born

    The idea of Pen Gu Island Tours began at the last Big Z memorial surf contest. It was the year that Reggie Belafonte was released as the coordinator and the surf contest was disbanded. Cody Maverick took to heart Big Z's philosophy and began creating what we have today!

  • June 2008 - Present

    New Beginnings

    During this year wait, Cody built from scratch Pen Gu Island Tours, or PGI Tours. He was able to build the team of guides that we have today. In June of 2008, exactly a year later, they officially opened PGI Tours. They served their first customers and continue serving thousands of people everyday.
